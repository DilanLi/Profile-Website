About

Born and raised in China, Dilan moved to Utah at the age of 14. She enjoys books,reptiles, hiking, traveling and good food.

During college, Dilan's pursuit of visual aesthtics led her onto the journey of becoming an illustrator. Along with drawing and sketching, she accquired other designing skills such as Adobe illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, etc.

After college, her inner tendency of structure led to an interest for Web Development. In this process of becoming a web developer, Dilan wants to apply her sensitivity with design into the frontend, while developing the backend knowledge to support a smooth, interactive web experience.

By the end of the program, Dilan will be ready to develope both the frontend and backend of a website by scratch. More importantly, she will be ready to support a professional team wherever her skills become most useful.
