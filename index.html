About Me

Born and raised in China, Dilan moved to Utah at the age of 14. She enjoys books,reptiles, hiking, traveling and good food. During college, Dilan's pursuit of visual aesthtics lead her onto the journey of becoming an illustrator. Along with drawing and sketching, she accquired other designing skills such as Adobe illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, etc. After college, her inner tendency of structure developed into an interest for Web Development. In this process of becoming a web developer, Dilan's goal is to apply her sensitivity with design into the frontend, while developing the backend knowledge to support a smooth, interactive web experience. By the end of the program, Dilan will be ready to individually develope professionally branded websites by scratch, but more importantly, supporting her team with wherever her skills become most useful.
